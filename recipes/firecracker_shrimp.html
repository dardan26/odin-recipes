Firecracker Shrimp

Description:

To make these easy firecracker shrimp, simply toss fresh shrimp in a sweet and spicy Asian-inspired sauce and then quickly stir fry in a wok.

This homemade firecracker shrimp recipe, developed by culinary expert Juliana Hale, is a sweet and spicy flavor sensation.

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Salt
  • Sweet chili sauce
  • Lime zest
  • Lime juice
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil
  • Rice
  • Garnishes

Steps:

  1. Make the sauce.
  2. Dry the shrimp, season, and coat with cornstarch.
  3. Cook the shrimp in oil until opaque, then remove from the wok.
  4. Boil the sauce until thick.
  5. Return the shrimp to the wok and toss in the sauce.
  6. Serve the firecracker shrimp over rice and garnish.
